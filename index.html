ZANGO YOUTH VOLUNTEERS ASSOCIATION - ZAYVAS

Zango Youth Volunteers Association is a registered organization under the Registrar General Department of Ghana, the ultimate aim is to serve our community by dedicating ourselves towards providing feasible and sustainable solutions to the challenges faced by the Community, the Youths and the Nation at large and to change the perception of the world, on the meaning of Zango and how the people from Zango communities are seen in the outside world by allowing young people from the community to exercise their right to participate (to be involved, to lead and to take action) in improving their health and well-being around the world. Participation means being actively involved in the planning, implementation and evaluation of development activities, programme and policies that affect the lives of Vulnerable, Underprivileged, Voiceless, Marginalized or disadvantaged group and the community at large.
